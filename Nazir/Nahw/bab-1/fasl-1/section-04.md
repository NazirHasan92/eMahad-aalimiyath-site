#### Hissa B — Tarkeeb (parsing) ka tareeqa

"Tarkeeb" matlab — jumla ko **tod kar dikhana ke kaun sa lafz kya kaam kar raha**. Beginner ke liye yeh skill **bohot zaroori** hai.

**Pattern: Jumla Ismiya Khabariya**
> **زَیْدٌ عَالِمٌ** → "زَیْدٌ **مبتدا** عَالِمٌ **خبر** — مبتدا خبر مل کر **جملہ اسمیہ خبریہ** ہوا۔"

**Pattern: Jumla Fi'liya Khabariya**
> **عَلِمَ زَیْدٌ** → "عَلِمَ **فعل** زَیْدٌ **فاعل** — فعل فاعل مل کر **جملہ فعلیہ خبریہ** ہوا۔"

**Mental template:**
1. Pehla lafz pakdo → uska naam batao (Mubtada / Fi'l)
2. Doosra lafz pakdo → uska naam batao (Khabar / Fa'il)
3. Donon ko join karke → poori jumla ka qism batao

#### Hissa D — Jumla Insha'iya ki tareef (finally!)

p-08 par sirf naam aaya tha. Ab tareef:

**جملہ انشائیہ** = aisa jumla jiske kehne wale ko **sach ya jhoot nahi** keh sakte.

**Kyon?** Kyunke **"انشاء" ka matlab hai "kuch paida karna."** Yeh jumla bhi ek kaam ko **paida** karne ki baat karta hai — yani action create karta hai, kisi guzashta fact ki khabar nahi deta.

**Book ki misaal:**
- **اِضْرِبْ** (= "Zarb paida karo") — yani hukm de raha "maaro"
- Yeh "sach" ya "jhoot" kese keh sakein? Yeh to hukm hai!

**Comparison:**

| Khabariya | Insha'iya |
|-----------|-----------|
| Khabar deta (past/present/future fact) | Kaam **paida** karta (hukm, sawal, tamanna) |
| Sach/jhoot keh sakte | Sach/jhoot nahi keh sakte |
| **زَیْدٌ ضَرَبَ** ("Zaid ne maara" — sach ya jhoot?) | **اِضْرِبْ** ("Maaro!" — sach/jhoot kya?) |

#### Hissa E — Jumla Insha'iya ki 6 qismein

Book kehti **"چند قسمیں"** (kuch qismein) — yani yeh exhaustive list nahi, sirf common qismein.

**Memory trick — 6 qismein ko 3 paron mein soch:**

**Pair 1: Hukm wale**
1. **اَمْر (Amr)** — "Karo!" — **اِضْرِبْ** (tum maaro)
2. **نَہی (Nahi)** — "Mat karo!" — **لَا تَضْرِبْ** (tum mat maaro)

**Pair 2: Khwahish wale**
3. **تَمَنِّی (Tamanni)** — "Kaash..." (na-mumkin wish) — **لَیْتَ زَیْدًا حَاضِرٌ** (kaash Zaid hazir hota)
4. **تَرَجِّی (Tarajji)** — "Ummeed hai..." (mumkin wish) — **لَعَلَّ عَمْرًوا غَائِبٌ** (ummeed hai Amr ghaib hoga)

**Pair 3: Sawal aur muamla**
5. **اِسْتِفْہَام (Istefham)** — "Kya...?" — **هَلْ ضَرَبَ زَیْدٌ؟** (kya Zaid ne maara?)
6. **عُقُود (Uqood)** — Muamlat / contracts — **بِعْتُ وَاشْتَرَیْتُ** (maine becha aur kharida)

> **`[?]` ATTENTION**: Tamanni aur Tarajji ka farq — book kehti hai "Tamanni = wish" aur "Tarajji = ummeed." Common samajh: **Tamanni** = aisi wish jo mumkin nahi (kaash maazi badal jaye!), **Tarajji** = aisi wish jo mumkin hai. Lekin book ne yeh explicit nahi kaha — sirf لیت aur لعل ke alfaaz ke saath misaal di hai. Apne shaykh se confirm karna.

> **Footnote (incomplete) ki ahmiyat** — **بِعْتُ وَاشْتَرَیْتُ** **dekhne mein** Fi'liya Khabariya hai (Fi'l + chhupa hua Fa'il "main"). Lekin **kharidari ke waqt** jab kaha jaata hai, to woh actually contract create kar raha hota hai (Insha'iya kaam). Footnote yeh nuance explain karta hai but text incomplete. **Mukammal text p-10 par.**

| Context | Kya hai | Reference |
