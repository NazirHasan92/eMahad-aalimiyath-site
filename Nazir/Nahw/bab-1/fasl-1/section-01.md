### Aaj ki kahani (page ka khulasa — pedagogical narrative)

Yeh book ka pehla content page hai aur bohot zyada cheezein ek saath aati hain. Don't panic — hum step-by-step samjhenge.

#### Hissa A — Muqaddama (3 boxes: Tareef, Faida, Mauzu' Lahu)

Musannif sahab pehle bata rahe hain ke علم نحو kya cheez hai aur kyon parhna hai. Teen sawal answer hote hain:

1. علم نحو ki tareef (definition) — kya hai?

> *"Arabi zabaan mein, woh ilm jo do cheezein sikhata hai:*
> *(a) اسم، فعل، حرف ko jod kar jumla banane ki tarteeb,*
> *(b) Aur har kalimah ke aakhri harf ki halat (haalat = zer/zabar/pesh/jazm — yeh aage i'raab kehlayega)."*Analogy: Soch — agar koi ladka aapko alag-alag bricks de aur kahe "ghar bana lo," to aap kya karenge?
- Aap pehle samjhenge kaunsi brick kahan lagti hai (foundation, wall, roof).
- Phir samjhenge bricks ko kaise jodte hain (cement, alignment).

Wahi Arabi mein: Aapko alfaz (اسم، فعل، حرف) milte hain. علم نحو aapko sikhata hai:
- Inhe jod kar jumla kaise banta hai (tarteeb).
- Aur har lafz ke aakhri harf par kya nishani lagani hai (zer / zabar / pesh / jazm — yeh "حالت" hai).

2. فائدہ (benefit) — kyon parhein?

> *"Taake insan Arabi bolne aur likhne mein har qism ki ghalti se mahfooz rahe."*

Misaal jo book deti hai: زَیْدٌ، دَارٌ، دَخَلَ، فِيْ — yeh 4 alag alfaz hain. علم نحو aapko sikhata hai:
- In 4 ko mila kar ek sahih jumla kaise banta hai.
- Aur usko sahih tareeqe se ada kaise karna hai (kaun sa lafz pehle, kaun sa baad mein, kis par kya zer-zabar).

> Note: Book yahan jumla nahi banake dikhati — sirf example deti hai ke "yeh 4 alfaz hain." Asal jumla banana aage seekhenge.

3. موضوع لہ (subject matter) — kis cheez ki baat hoti hai is fan mein?

> *"Is ilm ka mauzu' hai: کلمہ aur کلام."*

Yaani — poori book in 2 cheezon ke gird ghoomegi: Kalimah (lafz) aur Kalam (mukammal baat). Isi liye Fasl 1 ka naam bhi yahi hai: کلمہ وکلام.

---

#### Hissa B — Fasl 1 ka asal content (Lafz → Mufrad/Murakkab → Kalimah → Ism)

Yeh ek definition tree hai jo top-to-bottom utarta hai. Soch — ek bara box jisme chhota box, jisme aur chhota box.

Step 1 — لفظ (Lafz) kya hai?
> *"Jo baat aadmi ki zabaan se nikle, usko لفظ kehte hain."*

Yaani koi bhi awaaz jo muh se nikle. Chahe kuch matlab rakhti ho ya nahi. Misaal aapke around: "Salam," "kitab," "blah blah" — sab "lafz" hain (kyunke awaaz muh se nikli).

Step 2 — Lafz ki 2 qismein: مَوضُوع aur مُہمل
- مَوضُوع (Mauzu') — wo lafz jo bamaani ho (matlab rakhta ho). Misaal: "kitab."
- مُہمل (Muhmal) — wo lafz jo bematlab ho. Misaal: "blah," "xyz."

| Lafz | Bamaani? | Naam |
|------|----------|------|
| کِتَاب (kitaab) | Haan | مَوضُوع |
| دَیلْز (random gibberish) | Nahi | مُہمل |

علم نحو sirf لفظ موضوع par baat karta hai. Muhmal se humein matlab nahi.

Step 3 — Lafz Mauzu' ki 2 qismein: مُفرد aur مُرَکَّب
- مُفرد (Mufrad) — akela lafz jo ek matlab batata ho.
- مُرَکَّب (Murakkab) — kayi alfaz ka jod (compound).

Step 4 — Mufrad ko hi "کَلِمَہ" kehte hain!
> *"Mufrad: us akele lafz ko kehte hain jo ek maani batata ho, aur isi ko 'کلمہ' bhi kehte hain."*Yeh bohot important hai: "Kalimah" aur "Mufrad" ek hi cheez hain. Yani jab kahen "kalimah," matlab "ek akela bamaani lafz."

Step 5 — Kalimah ki 3 qismein:
1. اسم (Ism) — naam ka lafz (noun)
2. فعل (Fi'l) — kaam ka lafz (verb)
3. حرف (Harf) — jodne wala lafz (particle)

> Yahan se "Ism / Fi'l / Harf" ki tareefein shuru hoti hain — pehli aati hai Ism ki.

Step 6 — Ism ki tareef (book ki exact tareef):
> *"اسم woh kalimah hai jiske maani doosre kalimah ke milaaye baghair maloom ho jaayen, aur usme teenon zamano (Maazi, Haal, Mustaqbil) mein se koi zamana na paaya jaaye, jaise: رَجُلٌ، عِلْمٌ، مِفْتَاحٌ۔"*Yeh tareef ko todte hain:
- Pehli shart: Ism akela samjha ja sake — doosre lafz ke saath jodne ki zaroorat nahi.
- Doosri shart: Ism mein time / zamana nahi hota.

Examples (book se verbatim):
- رَجُلٌ (Rajulun) — aadmi
- عِلْمٌ ('Ilmun) — ilm
- مِفْتَاحٌ (Miftaahun) — chaabi

In teenon ko aap akele bolen — samajh aa jata hai (aadmi, ilm, chaabi). Aur in mein koi waqt (past/present/future) nahi hai.

Counter (taqabul): "ضَرَبَ" (zaraba — usne maara) — yeh Ism nahi hai. Kyon? Kyunke is mein zamana hai (maazi — past). Yeh Fi'l hai (aage aayega).

Step 7 — Ism ki 3 qismein (page ke aakhri line — sirf naming, tareef agle page par):
1. جامد (Jamid) — patthar jaisa, jo kisi se nikla nahi.
2. مصدر (Masdar) — woh ism jis se aur alfaz banti hain.
3. مشتق (Mushtaq) — Masdar se bana hua ism.

> Yeh tareefein abhi yahan nahi hain. Sirf naam diye gaye hain. Tafseel agle page par.

### Yaad rakho (page ke 5 mukhya cheezein)

1. علم نحو ki tareef = jod kar jumla banane ki tarteeb + har kalimah ke aakhri harf ki haalat.
2. علم نحو ka mauzu' = Kalimah aur Kalam.
3. Lafz ki 2 qismein: Mauzu' (bamaani) + Muhmal (bematlab). Hum sirf Mauzu' par baat karte hain.
4. Lafz Mauzu' ki 2 qismein: Mufrad + Murakkab. Mufrad = Kalimah (same cheez).
5. Kalimah ki 3 qismein: Ism + Fi'l + Harf. Pehla — Ism — wo lafz jo akela samjha ja sake aur jisme zamana na ho. Misaal: رَجُلٌ، عِلْمٌ، مِفْتَاحٌ.
