### Aaj ki kahani (page ka khulasa — pedagogical narrative)

p-06 par humne **naam** liye the (Jamid, Masdar, Mushtaq, Fi'l, Harf, Murakkab). p-07 par har ek ki **proper tareef** aati hai. Aur sabse aakhir mein — **Bab 1 ka title "Kalam" finally define hota hai**.

#### Hissa A — Ism ki 3 qismein ki tareefein (Jamid, Masdar, Mushtaq)

Yaad karo: **Ism** = woh kalimah jo akela samjha ja sake aur jisme zamana na ho. Ab Ism khud 3 qismein mein bantta hai:

**1. جامد (Jamid)** — "patthar jaisa"
- **Tareef**: Aisa Ism jo **na kisi se bana ho aur na is se kuch bante**.
- **Misaal**: **رَجُلٌ** (aadmi — *meri taraf se gloss; book mein nahi*), **فَرَسٌ** (ghoda — *meri taraf se gloss; book mein nahi*)
- **Analogy:** Ek **akela patthar** soch — woh na kisi cheez se bana hai, na us se kuch banta hai. Bas hai. "Rajulun" aisa hi hai — bas "aadmi" hai, kisi se nikla nahi, na is se kuch nikalta.

**2. مصدر (Masdar)** — "fountain / source"
- **Tareef**: Aisa Ism jo **khud kisi se nahi banta**, magar **us se bohot lafz bante hain**.
- **Misaal**: **نَصْرٌ** (madad karna — *meri taraf se gloss; book mein nahi*), **ضَرْبٌ** (maarna — *meri taraf se gloss; book mein nahi*)
- **Analogy:** Ek **chashma (spring)** soch — pani usi se nikalta hai, magar woh khud kisi se nahi banta. Yeh Masdar hai — source. *(Analogy meri taraf se — book mein nahi.)*

**3. مشتق (Mushtaq)** — "derived"
- **Tareef**: Aisa Ism jo **Masdar se bana ho**.
- **Misaal**:
  - **ضَرْبٌ** se → **ضَارِبٌ** (maarne wala — *meri taraf se gloss; book mein nahi*)
  - **نَصْرٌ** se → **نَاصِرٌ** (madad karne wala — *meri taraf se gloss; book mein nahi*)
- **Analogy:** Yeh chashme se nikla pani hai — Masdar source tha, Mushtaq us se "nikla" hai. *(Analogy meri taraf se — book mein nahi.)*

> **Connection:** Masdar aur Mushtaq ka rishta — Masdar source hai, Mushtaq us se bane huye hain. Jamid donon mein se kuch nahi — alone aur akela.

#### Hissa B — Fi'l ki tareef (kalimah ki doosri qism)

**فعل (Fi'l)** — "kaam"
- **Tareef**: Wo kalimah jiska matlab **doosre lafz ke baghair samajh aa jaye** (yahan tak Ism jaisa) **AUR jisme koi zamana paaya jaye** (yahan Ism se farq).
- **Misaal**: **نَصَرَ** (madad ki — *meri taraf se gloss; book mein nahi*), **یَنْصُرُ** (madad karta hai — *meri taraf se gloss*), **ضَرَبَ** (maara — *meri taraf se gloss*), **یَضْرِبُ** (maarta hai — *meri taraf se gloss*)

**Yeh Ism aur Fi'l mein BUNIYADI farq hai:**

| Cheez | Ism | Fi'l |
|-------|-----|------|
| Akela samjha jaye? | Haan | Haan |
| Zamana (time)? | **Nahi** | **Haan** |
| Misaal | رَجُلٌ، عِلْمٌ | نَصَرَ، یَنْصُرُ |

**Fi'l ki 4 qismein** (sirf naam — tareefein **Sarf book** mein hain):
1. **ماضی (Maazi)** — past
2. **مضارع (Muzaari')** — present/future
3. **امر (Amr)** — command
4. **نہی (Nahi)** — prohibition

> **Bohot mufeed cross-reference:** Book khud kehti hai *"ان کی تعریفیں علم الصرف میں بیان ہو چکی ہیں"* — yani Sarf book parhne wala student yahan se Sarf ke notes consult karega. Yeh book deliberately Sarf ke baad parhi jati hai.

#### Hissa C — Harf ki tareef (kalimah ki teesri qism)

**حرف (Harf)** — "jodne wala lafz"
- **Tareef**: Wo kalimah jiska matlab **doosre kalimah ke baghair samajh nahi aata**.
- **Misaal**: **مِنْ** (se), **فِيْ** (mein)
- **Wajah**: Jab tak in huroof ke saath koi Ism ya Fi'l na ho, in se mustaqil maani nahi nikalte.

**Misaal-e-jumla (book ke Arabic; Urdu tarjuma meri taraf se):**
- **خَرَجَ زَیْدٌ مِنَ الدَّارِ** — "Zaid ghar se nikla" *(tarjuma meri taraf se — book mein nahi)*
  - Yahan **مِنْ** akela bematlab tha, لیکن جب "Zaid" aur "Daar" se jura, to maani aaye.
- **دَخَلَ عَمْرٌو فِي الْمَسْجِدِ** — "Amr masjid mein dakhil hua" *(tarjuma meri taraf se — book mein nahi)*
  - Yahan **فِيْ** ne "Amr" aur "Masjid" ko joda.

**Yeh Ism/Fi'l aur Harf mein farq:**
- Ism/Fi'l = **akela samjha ja sakta hai** (Rajulun = "aadmi" akela samjho)
- Harf = **akela samajh nahi aata** (Min = "se" — kis se? Akele meaningless)

**Harf ki 2 qismein** (sirf naam yahan):
1. **عامل (Amil)** — kaam karne wala (matlab kisi par i'rab dalta hai)
2. **غیر عامل (Ghair Amil)** — jo i'rab na dale

> Tareefein baad mein aayengi.

#### Hissa D — Murakkab ki tareef + asal climax: Kalam ki tareef

Ab waapas chalte hain p-06 ki tree par. Yaad hai? **Lafz Mauzu' ki 2 qismein** thin: **Mufrad + Murakkab**. Mufrad ko humne mukammal kiya (Ism/Fi'l/Harf). Ab **Murakkab**:

**۲۔ مرکب (Murakkab)** — "joda hua"
- **Tareef**: Wo lafz jo **do ya zyada kalimon ko jod kar** banaya jaye.
- Yani jab 2 ya zyada kalimah mile, woh Murakkab kehlata hai.

**Murakkab ki 2 qismein:**
1. **مفید (Mufid)** — fayda dene wala
2. **غیر مفید (Ghair Mufid)** — fayda na dene wala (tareef agle page par)

**مرکب مفید (Murakkab Mufid)** — yeh page ka **asal climax** hai!
- **Tareef**: Aisa Murakkab ke jab kehne wala apni baat **mukammal** kar le, to sunne wale ko:
  - **Ya** kisi guzashta واقعہ ki **khabar** maloom ho jaye, **ya**
  - Kisi baat ki **talab** (request/command) maloom ho jaye.

**2 Misaalein (book se):**

| Jumla | Tarjuma (book ka) | Kya khabar/talab? |
|-------|---------------------|---------------------|
| **ذَهَبَ زَیْدٌ** | "Zaid gaya" | Khabar — Zaid ke jaane ki |
| **اِئْتِ بِالْمَاءِ** | "Paani la" | Talab — kehne wala paani maang raha |

**Aur ab CLIMAX — Bab 1 ka title complete hua:**

> **"مرکب مفید کو 'جملہ' اور 'کلام' بھی کہتے ہیں۔"**

Yani:
**Murakkab Mufid = Jumla = Kalam** — **3 naam, ek hi cheez!**

> **Yaad karo:** Fasl 1 ka title tha **کلمہ وکلام** (Kalimah o Kalam). p-06 par **کلمہ** ki tareef mili thi (= Mufrad). Aaj p-07 par finally **کلام** ki tareef mil gayi (= Murakkab Mufid). **Bab 1 ka mauzu' yahan logically complete hua.**

### Yaad rakho (page ke 5 mukhya cheezein)

1. **Ism ki 3 qismein** ab definitions ke saath:
   - **Jamid** = patthar (na kisi se bana, na kuch is se bante)
   - **Masdar** = source (kuch nikalta is se, magar khud kuch se nikla nahi)
   - **Mushtaq** = Masdar se nikla
2. **Fi'l ki tareef** = akela samjha jaye AUR zamana ho. 4 qismein (Maazi/Muzaari/Amr/Nahi) ki tareef **Sarf book** mein.
3. **Harf ki tareef** = akela bematlab; doosre lafz ke saath maani aate hain. 2 qismein (Amil/Ghair Amil).
4. **Murakkab** = 2 ya zyada kalimon ka jod. 2 qismein: Mufid / Ghair Mufid.
5. **Murakkab Mufid = Jumla = Kalam** — 3 naam ek hi cheez! Yeh wahi "Kalam" hai jo Bab ke title mein hai.
