### Aaj ki kahani (page ka khulasa — pedagogical narrative)

p-07 par humne dekha tha — **Jumla = Murakkab Mufid = Kalam**. Ab book Jumla ko **khol kar** dikhati hai. Soch — humne ek bara box (Jumla) banaya, ab us ke andar chhote boxes dekhne hain.

#### Hissa A — Jumla ki 2 buniyadi qismein

Jumla 2 tarah ka hota hai:

| Qism | Khaas baat |
|------|------------|
| **جملہ خبریہ (Khabariya)** | Aisi baat jiske bare mein "**sach**" ya "**jhoot**" keh sakein |
| **جملہ انشائیہ (Insha'iya)** | (Tareef p-08 par nahi — aage aayegi) |

**Khabariya ka concept:** Agar koi kahe "Zaid school gaya" — to aap keh sakte hain "haan sach" ya "nahi jhoot." Yeh **Khabariya** hai.
**Insha'iya (preview):** Jaise hukm ya sawal — un par sach/jhoot nahi keh sakte. Tareef baad mein.

#### Hissa B — Jumla Khabariya ki 2 qismein: Ismiya / Fi'liya

Yeh **bohot important rule** hai:

> **Jumla ka pehla hissa kya hai — Ism ya Fi'l? — yeh决定 karta hai ke jumla Ismiya hai ya Fi'liya.**

| Jumla ki qism | Pehla hissa | Doosra hissa |
|----------------|-------------|----------------|
| **جملہ اسمیہ (Ismiya)** | **اسم** (must) | اسم ya فعل (kuch bhi) |
| **جملہ فعلیہ (Fi'liya)** | **فعل** (must) | فاعل (Ism, jo kaam karne wala) |

**Book ki misaalein — bohot dhyan se dekho:**

| Jumla | Pehla hissa | Doosra hissa | Qism |
|-------|-------------|----------------|------|
| **زَیْدٌ عَالِمٌ** | زَیْدٌ (Ism) | عَالِمٌ (Ism) | **Ismiya Khabariya** |
| **زَیْدٌ عَلِمَ** | زَیْدٌ (Ism) | عَلِمَ (Fi'l) | **Ismiya Khabariya** ← **Surprise!** |
| **عَلِمَ زَیْدٌ** | عَلِمَ (Fi'l) | زَیْدٌ (Fa'il) | **Fi'liya Khabariya** |
| **سَمِعَ بَکْرٌ** | سَمِعَ (Fi'l) | بَکْرٌ (Fa'il) | **Fi'liya Khabariya** |

> **Bohot ajeeb cheez:** **زَیْدٌ عَلِمَ** aur **عَلِمَ زَیْدٌ** — **same matlab** ("Zaid ne jaana") magar **alag qism**!
> - Pehla wala **Ismiya** hai (kyunke shuru Ism se hua)
> - Doosra wala **Fi'liya** hai (kyunke shuru Fi'l se hua)
>
> Yeh Arabic ki **tarteeb-ki-ahmiyat** ka pehla taste hai.

#### Hissa C — 4 nayi metalanguage terms

Yahan se Nahw ki asal terminology shuru hoti hai. **4 terms 2 pairs mein dekho:**

**Pair 1: مسند الیہ vs مسند (general terms)**

| Term | Tareef | Roman | Mental hook |
|------|--------|-------|--------------|
| **مسند الیہ (Musnad Ilayh)** | Jis ki taraf nisbat ki jaye | "Jis par describe ho raha" | Subject / topic |
| **مسند (Musnad)** | Jo nisbat ki jaye (description khud) | "Jo describe kar raha" | Predicate / news |

**Analogy:** Kissi ne kaha "**Zaid** **aalim** hai."
- "Zaid" — yeh wo **shakhs** hai jis ke bare mein hum baat kar rahe (= Musnad Ilayh)
- "Aalim" — yeh wo **khasiyat/news** hai jo us shakhs ke bare mein bata rahe (= Musnad)

**Yaani: Musnad Ilayh = topic ; Musnad = comment.**

**Pair 2: Ismiya jumlay mein — Mubtada / Khabar**

| Mubtada (مبتدا) | = Musnad Ilayh | Pehla hissa Ismiya mein | "Ibtida" se — jumla shuru hota |
| Khabar (خبر) | = Musnad | Doosra hissa Ismiya mein | Pehle Ism ke "haal ki khabar" deta |

**Misaal:** **زَیْدٌ عَالِمٌ**
- زَیْدٌ = Mubtada = Musnad Ilayh
- عَالِمٌ = Khabar = Musnad

**Pair 3: Fi'liya jumlay mein — Fi'l / Fa'il**

| Fi'l (فعل) | = Musnad | Pehla hissa Fi'liya mein | Yeh kaam khud hai |
| Fa'il (فاعل) | = Musnad Ilayh | Doosra hissa Fi'liya mein | Jis ne kaam kiya |

**Misaal:** **عَلِمَ زَیْدٌ**
- عَلِمَ = Fi'l (yahan element bhi Fi'l, term bhi Fi'l) = Musnad
- زَیْدٌ = Fa'il = Musnad Ilayh

> **Wait! Fi'l ka double meaning:** "Fi'l" pehle Kalimah ki ek qism thi (Ism/Fi'l/Harf). Yahan Jumla Fi'liya mein "Fi'l" us element ka naam bhi hai jo pehla hissa banta hai. **Same word — context decides matlab.**

#### Hissa D — Ism vs Fi'l: Musnad ya Musnad Ilayh?

Yeh aakhri 2 blocks par bohot khaas point banaya gaya hai:

| Element | Musnad ban sakta? | Musnad Ilayh ban sakta? |
|---------|---------------------|---------------------------|
| **اسم (Ism)** | ✅ Haan | ✅ Haan |
| **فعل (Fi'l)** | ✅ Haan | ❌ **NAHI** |

**Wajah** (logical):
- Ism kisi cheez ka **naam** hai. Aap naam ke bare mein khabar de sakte hain (Musnad Ilayh), ya naam ke through khabar de sakte hain (Musnad). Donon kaam.
- Fi'l ek **action** hai. Aap action ke bare mein khabar nahi dete — action **khud** khabar hai. Isi liye sirf Musnad.

**Book ki misaalein recap:**
- **زَیْدٌ عَالِمٌ** → دونوں Ism. Zaid = Musnad Ilayh, Aalim = Musnad.
- **زَیْدٌ عَلِمَ** → Pehla Ism (Zaid = Musnad Ilayh), doosra Fi'l (Alima = Musnad).
- **عَلِمَ زَیْدٌ** → Pehla Fi'l (Alima = Musnad), doosra Ism (Zaid = Musnad Ilayh).

**Yaad rakho:** Fi'l kahin bhi ho — pehle ya baad — Musnad Ilayh **nahi** ban sakta.

**"ہے، ہوں، ہو" wala point** (Block 3 se):
> *"جملہ اسمیہ خبریہ کے معنوں میں 'ہے، ہوں، ہو' آتا ہے۔"*

Yani Urdu mein jab Arabic Ismiya jumla translate karte hain, to "hai/hain/hon" ka lafz daalna parta hai jo Arabic mein nahi hota:
- **زَیْدٌ عَالِمٌ** — Urdu: "Zaid aalim **hai**" (Arabic mein "hai" nahi, magar matlab mein chhupa hai)

### Yaad rakho (page ke 5 mukhya cheezein)

1. **Jumla ki 2 qismein**: Khabariya (sach/jhoot keh sakein) + Insha'iya (tareef baad mein).
2. **Khabariya ki 2 qismein**: **Ismiya** (pehla Ism) + **Fi'liya** (pehla Fi'l + doosra Fa'il).
3. **Tarteeb se qism decide hoti hai**: زَیْدٌ عَلِمَ = Ismiya (Ism pehle), عَلِمَ زَیْدٌ = Fi'liya (Fi'l pehle) — **matlab same, qism alag**.
4. **4 metalanguage terms** — 2 paron mein:
   - General: **Musnad** (description) / **Musnad Ilayh** (topic)
   - Ismiya context: **Mubtada** (= Musnad Ilayh) / **Khabar** (= Musnad)
   - Fi'liya context: **Fi'l** (= Musnad) / **Fa'il** (= Musnad Ilayh)
5. **Bohot khaas qaida**: Ism donon ban sakta (Musnad ya Musnad Ilayh), **Fi'l sirf Musnad ban sakta** — kabhi Musnad Ilayh nahi.
