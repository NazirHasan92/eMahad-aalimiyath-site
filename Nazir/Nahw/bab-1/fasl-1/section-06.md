### Aaj ki kahani (page ka khulasa — pedagogical narrative)

#### Hissa A — Bina'i ka khaatma + harakaat rule

Mukammal Bina'i tareef (p-10 + p-11 combined):
> Aisa Murakkab ke 2 ism ek banaye gaye hon, dono mein koi nisbat اضافی (idafat) ya اسنادی (mubtada-khabar / fi'l-fa'il) na ho, aur pehle ism ko doosre se jodne wala koi harf bhi na ho.

Misaal step-by-step:
1. اَحَدٌ وَعَشَرٌ = "ek aur dus" (2 alag ism, beech mein و)
2. "و" hata diya + dono ism ko jod kar ek bana diya
3. = اَحَدَ عَشَرَ (gyaarah)

Same pattern: تِسْعَۃٌ وَعَشَرٌ → تِسْعَۃَ عَشَرَ (unees).

Bina'i ki harakaat rule (important!):
- Dono ism par hamesha فتح (fatha — zabar) rehti hai.
- Apwaad (exception): اِثْنَا عَشَرَ ("baarah") — is ka pehla hissa badalta rehta hai.

Yeh kya matlab "Mabni"? Yaad rakho — "Bina'i" naam isi liye hai ke yeh بنایا گیا (constructed) hai aur سرف نہیں چلتا (declension nahi badalti) — hamesha fatha par stuck.

#### Hissa B — Mana' Sarf ki tareef (3rd qism)

Tareef: Aisa Murakkab ke 2 ism ek kar liye gaye hon, aur rabt-dene wala koi harf na ho.

> Bina'i aur Mana' Sarf ki similarity — bohot subtle farq:
>
> | Feature | Bina'i | Mana' Sarf |
> |---------|--------|-------------|
> | 2 ism ek kiye? | ✅ | ✅ |
> | Rabt-wala harf? | ❌ Nahi | ❌ Nahi |
> | Nisbat-e-idafi/isnadi? | ❌ Nahi | (book mention nahi) |
> | Harakaat | Dono hisson par fatha | Sirf pehla hissa maftooh |
> | Misaal | اَحَدَ عَشَرَ (11) | بَعْلَبَكُّ (city name) |

Asal farq harakaat aur word-type mein hai:
- Bina'i = numbers (11-19) — dono hisson par fatha
- Mana' Sarf = proper names (cities, places) — sirf pehla hissa maftooh

Book ki misaal: بَعْلَبَكُّ
- = Ek shehar ka naam (modern: Baalbek, Lebanon mein hai)
- 2 hisson se mil kar bana:
 - بَعْل — ek but (idol) ka naam tha
 - بَكٌّ — us shehar ke bani-shehar baadshah ka naam tha
- Donon ek kar diye gaye, beech mein koi harf nahi → بَعْلَبَكُّ
- Pehla hissa بَعْلَ maftooh hai (fatha)

#### Hissa C — Teeno qismon ke examples in jumlay (review)

Yeh paragraph showing how each Ghair Mufid qism kisi bare jumlay ke andar kya role play karta hai:

| Bara Jumla | Ghair Mufid hissa | Qism |
|------------|---------------------|------|

Important pedagogical point: Murakkab Ghair Mufid khud poora jumla nahi hota — magar woh bare jumlay ka hissa ban sakta hai (subject, object, etc.).

#### Hissa D — Tarkeeb of all 3 example jumlay

Yeh book ka bohot zaroori section hai — pehla case jab tarkeeb mein layered structure dikhayi gayi:

(a) غُلَامُ زَیْدٍ حَاضِرٌ — Tarkeeb step-by-step:

```
غُلَامُ — مضاف
زَیْدٍ — مضاف الیہ
 ↓ (mil kar)
غُلَامُ زَیْدٍ — مبتدا (compound Mubtada!)
حَاضِرٌ — خبر
 ↓ (mil kar)
poora jumla — جملہ اسمیہ خبریہ
```

Naya insight: Mudaf + Mudaf Ilayh ka jod ek "compound Mubtada" ban sakta hai. Yani Mubtada hamesha akela ism nahi hota — Murakkab Idafi bhi Mubtada ki jagah le sakta.

(b) جَاءَ اَحَدَ عَشَرَ رَجُلًا — Tarkeeb step-by-step:

```
جَاءَ — فعل
اَحَدَ عَشَرَ — مُمَیَّز (Mumayyaz) [`[?]` term — abhi tak defined NAHI]
رَجُلًا — تَمیِیز (Tamyeez) [`[?]` term — abhi tak defined NAHI]
 ↓ (mil kar)
اَحَدَ عَشَرَ رَجُلًا — فاعل (compound Fa'il!)
 ↓ (mil kar)
poora jumla — جملہ فعلیہ خبریہ
```

`[?]` Forward reference: Mumayyaz aur Tamyeez — yeh 2 nayi terms use hui hain bina tareef ke. Yeh aage Babs mein define hongi (probably Bab 3 ya 4 mein). Abhi sirf yaad rakhna ke jab number + counted-noun pattern ho (jaise "11 mard"), to number = mumayyaz, counted noun = tamyeez.

(c) اِبْرَاهِيْمُ سَاکِنُ بَعْلَبَكَّ — Tarkeeb step-by-step:

```
اِبْرَاهِيْمُ — مبتدا
سَاکِنُ — مضاف
بَعْلَبَكَّ — مضاف الیہ
 ↓ (mil kar)
سَاکِنُ بَعْلَبَكَّ — خبر (compound Khabar!)
 ↓ (mil kar)
poora jumla — جملہ اسمیہ خبریہ
```

Interesting case: بَعْلَبَكَّ Mana' Sarf hai but yahan Mudaf Ilayh ka role bhi play kar raha hai! Yaani 2 structures ek saath: Mana' Sarf (word-level structure) + Mudaf Ilayh (sentence-level role). Concepts overlap kar sakte hain.

### Yaad rakho (Fasl 1 ka khaatma — 5 mukhya cheezein)

1. Bina'i: Numbers 11-19 (aur similar). Donon hisson par hamesha fatha. Exception: اِثْنَا عَشَرَ.
2. Mana' Sarf: Proper names (shehron, etc.). Sirf pehla hissa maftooh. Misaal: بَعْلَبَكُّ، حَضْرَ مَوْتَ.
3. Ghair Mufid khud jumla nahi — magar bare jumlay ka hissa ban sakta hai (Mubtada, Khabar, Fa'il ka role).
4. Tarkeeb mein layering: Mudaf+Mudaf Ilayh ek compound Mubtada/Khabar ban sakta. Mumayyaz+Tamyeez ek compound Fa'il.
5. Fasl 1 ab MUKAMMAL — Lafz → Kalimah + Kalam ke saare branches taught. Bab 1 done. Agla page (p-12) shayad Fasl 2 shuru karega.
