### Aaj ki kahani (page ka khulasa — pedagogical narrative)

#### Hissa A — Fasl 3 ka kaam: Kalimah ki shanaakht ke signs

Hum ne ab tak seekha:
- **Fasl 1**: Kalimah ki 3 qismein (Ism / Fi'l / Harf) ki **تعریفein**
- **Fasl 2**: Jumla ki 2 taqseemein (Zaati 4 + Sifati 6) — jumla **dekhne** ke 2 angles

Lekin tareef yaad karna **alag** baat hai, aur **kisi diye huye Arabic lafz ko dekh kar pehchanna** ke yeh Ism hai ya Fi'l hai ya Harf — **alag practical hunar**. Iske liye **علامت** (alamat = sign, marker) ki zaroorat hai.

**Fasl 3 yeh practical toolkit deta**: Ism ki alamat + Fi'l ki alamat + Harf ki alamat (akhir wala kam, kyunke Harf chand mehdood hote hain). Aaj ka page **sirf Ism ki alamat (11)** ka tafsil deta.

**Aam zindagi se misaal**: Aap dur se ek aadmi dekhain — kaise pehchanaen ke woh doctor hai? Stethoscope galay mein, white coat, hospital ID badge — yeh sab "doctor ki alamat" hain. Agar inn mein se koi ek bhi dikhe to aap keh sakte "yeh shayad doctor hai." Bilkul aise hi: ek Arabic lafz mein ya zer aaye, ya alif laam shuru mein, ya tanween aakhir mein — to **Ism hai**. *(meri taraf se analogy; book mein nahi)*

#### Hissa B — Alamat #1-3: Word-edge markers (kinaaron par signs)

Pehli 3 alamat lafz ke **kinaaron** (start ya end) par dikhne wali physical markers hain:

| # | Alamat | Kahaan? | Book ki misaal |
|---|--------|---------|-----------------|
| 1 | **الف لام** (Alif Laam) | **Shuru** mein | اَلْحَمْدُ ("Al-hamdu" — Al + Hamd) |
| 2 | **حرف جر** (Harf-e-Jarr) | **Shuru** mein (alag harf) | بِزَیْدِ ("bi-Zaydi" — بِ + Zayd) |
| 3 | **تنوین** (Tanween) | **Aakhir** mein | زَیْدٌ ("Zaydun" — tanween-dammah) |

**Yaad rakhne ka tareeqa**:
- Alif Laam = "**al-**" jaisa English ka "**the**" — Ism ka determiner.
- Harf-e-Jarr = preposition jaisa "in / by / on / from" — yeh **alag harf** hota magar Ism ke saath chipak jata (بِزَیْدِ = "Zayd ke saath/se"). Note: yeh Harf khud Harf hai, magar **uske baad ka lafz Ism hai**.
- Tanween = lafz ke aakhir par double-harakah (دو زبر، دو زیر، دو پیش) — Ism per hi lagti.

**Important**: Yeh **physical, dikhne wali** alamat hain. Sirf shakal dekh kar pehchaan ho jati.

#### Hissa C — Alamat #4-5: Syntactic role markers (jumlay ke andar ka role)

Agle 2 alamat **lafz ki shakal** mein nahi, balke **jumlay mein uska role** dekhne par milti hain:

| # | Alamat | Tafseel | Book ki misaal |
|---|--------|---------|-----------------|
| 4 | **مسند الیہ** ho | Jumlay mein Musnad Ilayh ka role (Mubtada/Fa'il) | **زَیْدٌ** قَائِمٌ (zayd yahan Mubtada = Musnad Ilayh) |
| 5 | **مضاف** ho | Idafat structure mein pehla hissa | **غُلَامُ** زَیْدٍ (غُلَامُ yahan Mudaf) |

**Cross-reference**: Yeh dono concepts hum p-08 (Musnad Ilayh) aur p-10 (Mudaf) par seekh chuke. Yahan book unhein **Ism ki alamat** ke taur par re-use kar raha — yaani agar koi lafz Musnad Ilayh hai ya Mudaf hai, to woh **zaroor Ism hoga**.

#### Hissa D — Alamat #6-9: Morphological forms (Sarf ke aspects)

Yeh 4 alamat **Ism ki sub-shaklon** ki hain — yaani Ism ki morphological derivation ke specific forms. Yeh sab tareefein **Sarf** mein detailed milengi (forward refs):

| # | Alamat | Matlab (mukhtasr) | Book ki misaal |
|---|--------|-------------------|-----------------|
| 6 | **مُصَغَّر** (Musaghghar) | "Diminutive" — chhota / pyara form (Sarf mein tafseel) | **قُرَیْشٌ** (Quresh = small "Qarsh"), **رُجَیْلٌ** (Rujayl = small Rajul = "munna aadmi") |
| 7 | **منسوب** (Mansoob) | "Relative/attributive" — "X-wala" form (Sarf mein tafseel) | **بَغْدَادِيٌّ** ("Baghdadi" = Baghdad ka), **هِنْدِيٌّ** ("Hindi" = Hind se) |
| 8 | **تثنیہ** (Tathniya) | "Dual" — do ki shakal (Sarf mein tafseel) | **رَجُلَانِ** ("Rajulaani" = do mard) |
| 9 | **جمع** (Jam') | "Plural" — teen ya zyada ki shakal (Sarf mein tafseel) | **رِجَالٌ** ("Rijal" = mard log) |

**Asooli baat**: Yeh 4 alamat sirf **Ism** par hi lagti — Fi'l ya Harf in shaklon mein nahi ate. (Faida box mein Fi'l ki tathniya/jam' ka nuance aata — Hissa E mein dekhenge.)

**Forward-ref discipline**: Musaghghar/Mansoob/Tathniya/Jam' ki **tafseeli tareef Sarf book** mein milegi. Yahan sirf naam + misaal kafi hain pehchanne ke liye.

#### Hissa E — Alamat #10-11: Quality + Femininity markers

Aakhri 2 alamat:

| # | Alamat | Matlab | Book ki misaal |
|---|--------|--------|-----------------|
| 10 | **موصوف** (Mausoof) ho | "Described / qualified" — Ism jis ki **صفت** (adjective) di gayi ho | **رَجُلٌ** کَرِیْمٌ ("Karim aadmi" — yahan رَجُلٌ Mausoof hai, کَرِیْمٌ Sifat) |
| 11 | **تائے متحرک** (Taa'-e-Mutaharrika) us se mili ho | Lafz ke aakhir mein **harakat-wali ت** (taa marbuta with vowel) — feminine marker | **ضَارِبَۃٌ** ("Daariba" = striker fem — ت par fatha + tanween) |

**Note on Mausoof**: Yeh **Sifat** (صفت / adjective) ka correlate hai — agar koi lafz Mausoof hai (uski sifat di gayi ho), to woh **zaroor Ism hai**. Sifat khud bhi Ism hai (e.g., کَرِیْمٌ bhi Ism). Yaani is misaal mein **DONO lafz** (رَجُلٌ aur کَرِیْمٌ) Ism hain — Mausoof aur Sifat dono Ism hote hain. *(yeh observation meri taraf se; book ne is point ko explicit nahi kiya)*

**Note on Taa'-e-Mutaharrika**: Yeh ت **mutaharrika** (harakat wali) honi chahiye — yaani aakhir mein zer/zabar/pesh ke saath. Sirf saakin ت ya silent ت Ism ki alamat nahi (book ne yahan tafseel nahi di; Sarf mein milegi).

#### Hissa F — Faida box: Fi'l ki tathniya/jam' ka asal matlab (Fa'il ke aitebar se)

Yeh box ek bohot important misconception clear karta. Pehlay alamat #8 + #9 (Tathniya/Jam') padh kar laga ke yeh **sirf Ism** par lagti — magar humne Sarf mein dekha hoga ke Fi'l ki bhi tathniya/jam' "siyaghe" (forms) hote hain (فَعَلَا، یَفْعَلَانِ، فَعَلُوْا، یَفْعَلُوْنَ etc.).

To kya woh Fi'l ki tathniya/jam' Fi'l ko Ism nahi banati? **NAHI!**

**Book ki wazaahat**:
- Fi'l **خud** tathniya ya jam' nahi hota.
- Jo "Fi'l ke تَثنیہ wa جمع siyaghe" kehlate hain — woh **Faa'il (subject) ke aitebar** se hain. Yaani:
  - **فَعَلَا** = "Un **do** mardon ne (1 kaam) kiya"
  - **یَفْعَلَانِ** = "Woh **do** mard (1 kaam) karte hain"
  - **فَعَلُوْا** = "Un **sab** mardon ne (1 kaam) kiya"
  - **یَفْعَلُوْنَ** = "Woh **sab** mard (1 kaam) karte hain"

- Yaani **kaam to ek hi hua / ho raha**, magar **karne wale** do ya zyada thay.
- Book ka aakhri lafz: **"خوب ذہن نشین کر لیں"** — "is point ko achhi tarah dimaag mein bitha lo."

**Pedagogical takeaway**: Tathniya/Jam' alamat **Ism** ke liye hi as-an-identifier kaam karti — kyunke Ism mein "do/teen" ka concept lafz ki shakal mein hota. Fi'l ka tathniya/jam' superficial hai — woh actually Fa'il ki ginti reflect karta hai, na ke action ki.

### Yaad rakho (page ke 5 mukhya cheezein)

1. **علامت (Alamat)** = pehchaan ka sign. **Ism ki 11 alamat** hain — agar in mein se **koi ek bhi** lafz par dikhe to **wo lafz Ism hai**.
2. **3 word-edge alamat**: الف لام / حرف جر (dono shuru mein) + تنوین (aakhir mein) — yeh dikhne wali "physical" signs hain.
3. **2 syntactic role alamat**: مسند الیہ ho ya مضاف ho — yeh jumlay ke andar ka role check karna padta.
4. **4 morphological alamat** (Sarf forward-refs): مُصَغَّر، منسوب، تثنیہ، جمع — Ism ki sub-forms.
5. **2 quality alamat**: موصوف ho ya تائے متحرک mili ho — Sifat ka taluq, ya feminine taa.
6. **Faida ka takeaway**: Fi'l **khud** tathniya/jam' nahi hota — Fi'l ke "tathniya/jam' siyaghe" actually Fa'il (subject) ki ginti dikhate, action ki nahi. Ek hi kaam ho raha hota, magar karne wale do ya sab.
