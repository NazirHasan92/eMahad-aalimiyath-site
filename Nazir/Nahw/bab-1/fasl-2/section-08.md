#### Hissa B — Qism 2: مُعَلِّلَہ (Mu'allila) — *علت bayan karne wala jumla*Tareef (Block 2 se): Mu'allila wo jumla hai jo pehlay jumlay ki علت (reason / cause) bayan kare.

Book ki misaal — ek Hadith Shareef:
- Pehla jumla: لَا تَصُوْمُوْا فِيْ هٰذِهِ الْأَیَّامِ ("In dinon mein roza na rakhna")
- Doosra jumla: فَإِنَّهَا أَیَّامُ أَکْلٍ وَشُرْبٍ وَبِعَالٍ ("kyunke yeh khane, peene aur jaamaa ke din hain")

Analysis:
- Pehlay jumlay mein hukm diya (5 din roza mat rakhna)
- Doosra jumla us hukm ki علت (wajah) bata raha — kyunke yeh khushi ke din hain
- Yaani doosra jumla = Mu'allila ("علت bayan karne wala")

Aam zindagi se analogy: Maa kehti hai "Aaj bahar mat jao" (pehla jumla = hukm) "کیونکہ bahar bohot garmi hai" (doosra jumla = علت). Doosra jumla Mu'allila hai. *(meri taraf se analogy; book mein nahi)*

#### Hissa C — Qism 3: مُعْتَرِضَہ (Mu'tariza) — *darmiyaani interjection*Tareef (Block 3 se): Mu'tariza wo jumla hai jo do jumlon ke darmiyaan aaye, magar dono ke saath us ka koi syntactic taluq na ho — sirf interject karne ke liye aaya ho.

Book ki misaal — Imam Abu Hanifa ka fiqhi qol:
> قَالَ اَبُوْ حَنِيْفَۃَ رَحِمَهُ اللهُ: "النِّیَّۃُ فِي الْوُضُوْءِ لَیْسَتْ بِشَرْطٍ"
> ("Abu Hanifa (Allah un par raham kare) ne kaha: 'Wudu mein niyyat shart nahi'")

Analysis:
- Main jumla: قَالَ اَبُوْ حَنِيْفَۃَ... النِّیَّۃُ فِي الْوُضُوْءِ لَیْسَتْ بِشَرْطٍ (Abu Hanifa ne yeh kaha)
- Beech mein interjectionرَحِمَهُ اللهُ ("Allah un par raham kare")
- Yeh dua-jumla syntactically alag hai — pehlay ya baad ke jumlay se grammatical taluq nahi
- Yaani رَحِمَهُ اللهُ = Mu'tariza

Aam zindagi se misaal: "Mera dost — *Allah usay sehat de* — bohot beemar tha." Beech wala "Allah usay sehat de" = Mu'tariza, baqi sentence se grammatical taluq nahi. *(meri taraf se analogy; book mein nahi)*Important point: PDF ne رَحِمَهُ اللهُ ko Abu Hanifa ke naam ke baad include kiya hai — yeh sahabah/aaimah ke liye adab ki riwayat hai. Islamic content rule, maine bhi verbatim hi rakha.

#### Hissa D — Qism 4-5-6: Musta'nafa + Haliya + Ma'tufa

4. مستانفہ (Musta'nafa) — *nayi baat ka aaghaaz*:
- Tareef: Wo jumla jis se naya kalam shuru kiya jaye (no prior connection).
- Misaal: اَلْکَلِمَۃُ عَلٰی ثَلٰثَۃِ أَقْسَامٍ — agar yeh kisi conversation ka pehla jumla hai, to yeh Musta'nafa hai.
- Alternate naam: Iss ko "جملہ ابتدائیہ" (Jumla Ibtida'iyya) bhi kehte. *Dono naam barabar hain*.

> Note: Yeh same Arabic example (اَلْکَلِمَۃُ عَلٰی ثَلٰثَۃِ أَقْسَامٍ) p-12 Mubaiyana mein bhi tha! Lekin context alag tha — Mubaiyana mein yeh pehla jumla tha (jo doosre jumlay ne wazeh kiya). Musta'nafa mein yeh shuruati jumla hai (jo kisi se follow nahi). Same Arabic, alag role — context se decide hota hai. *(yeh observation meri taraf se; book ne explicit yeh compare nahi kiya)*5. حالیہ (Haliya) — *حال (state) waqi hone wala jumla*:
- Tareef (PDF verbatim): *"وہ جملہ ہے جو حال واقع ہو"* — yaani jumla jo kisi aur kalam ka حال (state / circumstance) ban kar aaye.
- Misaalجَاءَنِيْ زَیْدٌ وَهُوَ رَاکِبٌ ("Zaid mere paas aaya aur wo sawar tha")
 - Main action: Zaid aaya
 - Haal (state): woh sawar tha (jis halaat mein aaya)
 - "وَهُوَ رَاکِبٌ" = حالیہ jumla

6. معطوفہ (Ma'tufa) — *عطف ke zariye joda gaya*:
- Tareef: Wo jumla jo pehlay jumlay par عطف (joined by و/ thumma / fa, etc.) kiya jaye.
- Misaalجَاءَنِيْ زَیْدٌ وَذَهَبَ عَمْرُو ("Zaid mere paas aaya aur Amr chala gaya")
 - Pehla: جَاءَنِيْ زَیْدٌ (Zaid aaya)
 - Doosra: ذَهَبَ عَمْرُو (Amr chala gaya)
 - و (و atf-wala) se dono jude — doosra jumla = Ma'tufa

Haliya vs Ma'tufa mein bareek farq:
| | Haliya | Ma'tufa |
|--|--------|---------|
| Tareef | Action ke saath-saath kya tha (state) | Alag action jo "aur" se juda |
| Subject | Aksar same (وَهُوَ — "aur wo") | Aksar alag (وَذَهَبَ عَمْرُو — "aur Amr") |
| Misaal mein dono Arabic shabd start | وَهُوَ | وَذَهَبَ |

*(yeh farq mera observation hai — book ne yeh explicit comparison nahi diya; sirf 2 alag tareefein di hain)*

### Yaad rakho (page ke 5 mukhya cheezein)

1. Sifati taqseem ki 6 qismein: Mubaiyana / Mu'allila / Mu'tariza / Musta'nafa (= Jumla Ibtida'iyya) / Haliya / Ma'tufa.
2. Mubaiyana vs Mu'allila farq: dono pehlay jumlay par "kuch karte hain" — magar Mubaiyana wazeh karta (ambiguity door) jab keh Mu'allila علت batata (kyon ka jawab).
3. Mu'tariza ka pehchaan: jumla beech mein aaye magar pehlay/baad walay se koi grammatical taluq na ho. Misaal: رَحِمَهُ اللهُ (dua-jumla) Abu Hanifa quote ke beech.
4. Musta'nafa = جملہ ابتدائیہ — dono naam ek hi qism ke. *Nayi baat ka pehla jumla*.
5. Haliya vs Ma'tufa farq: Haliya = same subject ka haal (وَهُوَ رَاکِبٌ — "aur wo sawar tha"). Ma'tufa = alag subject ka alag action عطف se juda (وَذَهَبَ عَمْرُو — "aur Amr chala gaya").

> Fasl 1 ne Kalimah/Kalam samjhaaye (تعریفi taqseem). Fasl 2 ne Jumla ko 2 angles se taqseem kiya. Fasl 3 ek alag kaam karta — Kalimah ke 3 aqsam (Ism/Fi'l/Harf) ko pehchanna kaise — yaani har qism ki علامتیں (alamat = signs) deta. Yeh practical identification toolkit hai.
