### Aaj ki kahani (page ka khulasa — pedagogical narrative)

#### Hissa A — Fasl 2 ka angle: Jumla ki taqseem ka naya tariqa

Fasl 1 mein humne Jumla ko maa'ne ki bunyaad par dekha tha:
- Khabariya (sach/jhoot keh sakein) → Ismiya/Fi'liya
- Insha'iya (sach/jhoot na keh sakein) → 10 qismein

Fasl 2 ab Jumla ko 2 naye perspectives se dekhata:
1. بااعتبارِ ذات (Bā i'tibār-e-Zaat) — jumla ki khud ki banaawat (structure-by-essence) ke aitebar se. 4 qismein.
2. بااعتبارِ صفت (Bā i'tibār-e-Sifat) — jumla ki sifat / role ke aitebar se (ek jumla doosre jumlay ke saath kya kaam karta hai). 6 qismein.

اِعْتِبار (i'tibar) = "considering from a perspective". Aam zindagi se misaal: ek aadmi ko aap "*Doctor*" bhi keh sakte (peshay ke اعتبار se), "*Baap*" bhi keh sakte (rishte ke اعتبار se), "*Lambay qad wala*" bhi keh sakte (jismani sifat ke اعتبار se) — same aadmi, alag-alag اعتبار. *(meri taraf se analogy; book mein nahi)*Important structural question (Nazir ke liye flag): Fasl 1 ka Ismiya/Fi'liya (Khabariya ke sub-qismein) aur Fasl 2 ka Ismiya/Fi'liya (4 zaati qismein mein se 2) — kya same qismein hain? Misaalein dekhne se same lagti hain:
- Fasl 1 Ismiya misaal: زَیْدٌ عَالِمٌ
- Fasl 2 Ismiya misaal: زَیْدٌ قَائِمٌ — pattern same (Ism + Ism)
- Fasl 1 Fi'liya misaal: عَلِمَ زَیْدٌ
- Fasl 2 Fi'liya misaal: قَامَ زَیْدٌ — pattern same (Fi'l + Fa'il)

Lekin Fasl 2 ke baqi 2 zaati qismein — Shartiya aur Zarfiya — Fasl 1 mein nahi thi. Yeh naye qismein hain ya pichli framework mein chupi thi? Book aage clarify karega — abhi assume nahi karna.

#### Hissa B — Jumla = ≥2 kalimat (Block 1 ki taqseer)

Asooli qaidaKoi bhi jumla 2 kalimon se kam ka nahi ho sakta.

Yeh 2 kalimein 2 tareeqon se present ho sakti hain:

(1) لفظاً (lafzan) — alfaaz mein khulla maujood:
> ضَرَبَ زَیْدٌ ("Zaid ne maara")
- ضَرَبَ = Fi'l (pehla kalimah)
- زَیْدٌ = Ism (doosra kalimah)
- Donon dikhayi de rahe alfaaz ki shakal mein.

(2) تقدیراً (taqdeeran) — andar chupa hua / poshida:
> اِضْرِبْ ("(tu) maar!")
- اِضْرِبْ = Fi'l Amr (zaahir kalimah, "tu maar")
- اَنْتَ ("tu") = doosra kalimah, magar alfaaz mein nahi — implied / poshida hai
- Phir bhi yeh Fi'l ka Fa'il (kaam karne wala) hai — "tu" jo "maar raha hai"

Yaani: ek-lafz dikhne wala jumla (اِضْرِبْ) bhi asal mein 2 kalimon ka hai — bas doosra (اَنْتَ) hum khud andar samajh lete hain.

Upper limit? Nahi — jumla 2 se zyada kalimat bhi ho sakta hai, koi maximum nahi hai.

### Yaad rakho (page ke 5 mukhya cheezein)

1. Jumla ≥ 2 kalimat — kabhi 1 se nahi banta. 2 kalimein لفظاً (alfaaz mein, jaise ضَرَبَ زَیْدٌ) ya تقدیراً (poshida, jaise اِضْرِبْ mein اَنْتَ) maujood hoti hain.
2. Bā i'tibār-e-Zaat = 4 qismeinاسمیہ / فعلیہ / شرطیہ / ظرفیہ — yeh 4 = "اصل جملہ".
3. Bā i'tibār-e-Sifat = 6 qismein — heading aaya, pehli qism (مبینہ) ka shuru hua, baqi p-13 onwards.
4. Fasl 1 ka Ismiya/Fi'liya (Khabariya ke sub-qismein) aur Fasl 2 ka Ismiya/Fi'liya (zaati qismein) — examples se same lagte (`زَیْدٌ قَائِمٌ` vs `زَیْدٌ عَالِمٌ` jaisa). Lekin Shartiya/Zarfiya naye hain — book aage clarify karega.
5. Block 2 mein 4 forward-ref terms aaye (Mu'arrab / Mabni / Amil / Ma'mool) — book ne bataya ke jumla samajhne ke liye yeh sab pehchanna zaroori, magar tareefein abhi nahi mili. (Bab 4 expected per Mu'arrab/Mabni; Amil/Ma'mool agle pages par possibly.)
