### Aaj ki kahani (page ka khulasa — pedagogical narrative)

#### Hissa A — Page ka angle: "Kaunsi categories Mabni? Kaunsi Mu'arrab?"

Section 11 mein hum ne tareefein dekheen (Mu'arrab = badalta, Mabni = na badalta). Magar ek **practical sawal** raha: agar koi Arabic lafz dekho, **kaise pata chale ke woh Mu'arrab hai ya Mabni**?

Aaj ka page **3 alag tariqe** se yeh sawal ka jawab deta:

1. **Block 1**: Direct list — kaunsi categories Mabni hain (general rule)
2. **Block 5**: Inverse list — kaunsi categories Mu'arrab hain (sirf 2 hi)
3. **Block 7-8**: Conceptual basis — **Mabni-al-Asl** (3 original Mabni) + Ism Ghair Mutamakkin = un ke "mushabih" (resembling)

Yeh **2-tier Mabni framework** hai. Pehli baar yahan introduce hua. *(yeh "2-tier framework" framing meri taraf se — book ne explicit ko "2-tier" nahi kaha, magar Block 6-7 ka structure yahi suggest karta)*

#### Hissa B — Mu'arrab kaun? Sirf 2 categories (with conditions)

Pora khel summary mein clear hai (Block 5):

| # | Mu'arrab kaun | Shart (condition) |
|---|---------------|--------------------|
| 1 | **اسم متمکن** (Ism Mutamakkin) | **ترکیب** (jumla structure) mein ho — "Bashartike tarkeeb mein waaqe ho." Agar isolated, to Mabni. |
| 2 | **فعل مضارع** (Fi'l Muzaari) | **نون جمع مؤنث** aur **نون تاکید** **se khali** ho (yaani in 2 noon-suffix mein se koi nahi ho) |

**"Mutamakkin" naam kyon?** (Block 3): **تَمَکُّن** ka matlab hai **"jagah dena"** (to give place/space). Ism Mutamakkin **اعراب ko jagah deta** — uska aakhri harakah change ho sakta — isi liye yeh "place-giver" Mu'arrab hai. *(yeh "place-giver" Roman gloss meri taraf se; book "jagah dena" se aage explicit gloss nahi deta)*

**Forward-refs (Sarf terms — yahan sirf naam, tafseel Sarf book mein):**
- **نون جمع مؤنث** (Noon Jam' Mu'annath) — feminine plural noon suffix on Fi'l Muzaari
- **نون تاکید** (Noon Ta'keed) — emphasizing noon suffix
- **امر حاضر معروف** (Amr Haazir Ma'roof) — present-active command form

#### Hissa C — Mabni kaun? Block 1 ka list

PDF Block 1 ka explicit list (general rule):

| Category | Mabni status |
|----------|--------------|
| **Asma** mein | Sirf **اسم غیر متمکن** Mabni |
| **Af'aal** mein — **فعل ماضی** | Mabni |
| **Af'aal** mein — **امر حاضر معروف** | Mabni |
| **Af'aal** mein — **فعل مضارع** (with noon jam' mu'annath ya noon ta'keed) | Mabni |
| **Huroof** | **Tamam** Mabni |

**Big picture**: Mu'arrab woh hai jis ke 2 categories upar diye (Hissa B). Baqi sab Mabni hain. Yaani Mabni is the **default**, Mu'arrab is the **exception** in Arabic morphology. *(yeh "default vs exception" framing meri taraf se; book ne explicit yeh contrast nahi diya)*

#### Hissa D — Mabni-al-Asl ki 3 qismein (Block 7): Original cohort

Block 7 ek important concept introduce karta: **مبنی الاصل** (Mabni-al-Asl) = "original Mabni" — woh 3 cheezein jo **inherently** Mabni hain:

| # | Mabni-al-Asl kaun |
|---|---------------------|
| 1 | **فعل ماضی** (Fi'l Maazi) |
| 2 | **امر حاضر معروف** (Amr Haazir Ma'roof) |
| 3 | **تمام حروف** (All Huroof) |

**Yeh "original Mabni" kya special hain?** Yeh **bina kisi resemblance** ke Mabni hain — Mabni honay ki wajah unki khud ki nature hai, kisi aur ki nakal nahi. Compare to **Ism Ghair Mutamakkin** — woh **mushabih** (resembling) hone ki wajah se Mabni hai (Block 6).

**Loose analogy**: "Original" celebrity vs "celebrity-look-alike". Original = real-deal Mabni. Look-alike (Ism Ghair Mutamakkin) = Mabni because it resembles the originals. *(meri taraf se analogy; book mein nahi)*

#### Hissa E — Ism Ghair Mutamakkin = Mushabih + 4 tareeqein of mushabahat (Block 8 walkthrough)

**Block 6** says: **اسم غیر متمکن** = woh Ism jo **مبنی الاصل ka مشابہ** (resembling) ho.

**Block 8** detail karta: yeh "mushabahat" (resemblance) **4 tareeqon se** ho sakti — har tareeqe ki PDF ne ek-do misaal di:

| # | Resemblance ka tareeqa | Misaal (PDF verbatim) | Kis Mabni-al-Asl ke saath similarity? |
|---|----------------------|------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| 1 | **Mabni-al-Asl ke maa'na rakhe** (resembling by meaning) | **اَیْنَ** (kahaan?) | **ہمزۂ استفہام** (a Harf, Mabni-al-Asl) |
| 1 (cont.) | Same | **هَیْهَاتَ** | **ماضی** ka معنی (Maazi, Mabni-al-Asl) |
| 1 (cont.) | Same | **رُوَیْدَ** | **امر حاضر** ka معنی (Amr Haazir, Mabni-al-Asl) |
| 2 | **احتیاج** (need-based — jaise Harf kisi cheez ka muhtaaj hota, waise hi yeh Ism muhtaaj) | **اسمائے موصولہ** + **اسمائے اشارہ** | Mausoolah needs **صلہ** (relative clause); Ishara needs **مشار الیہ** (referent) — bilkul Harf jaisi "muhtaaji" |
| 3 | **3 letters se kam** (brevity — short like Huroof typically are) | **مَنْ، ذَا** | Huroof bhi aksar 2-3 letters ke hote |
| 4 | **Koi hissa Harf shamil ho** (contains a Harf in part) | **اَحَدَ عَشَرَ** (asal: **اَحَدٌ وَعَشَرٌ** — waw harf shamil tha) | Direct Harf inclusion |

**Note on examples**:
- **اَیْنَ** = "kahaan?" — PDF kehta hai is mein ہمزۂ استفہام ka maa'na hai (yaani sawaal poochne ka). Translation derivable from Hamza Istafham reference.
- **هَیْهَاتَ** — PDF sirf kehta "ماضی ke معنی" — actual gloss "doori, far away, be gone!" meri taraf se from Arabic vocab knowledge. `(meri taraf se gloss — "doori/far": book sirf "ماضی ka معنی" kehta, exact translation nahi deta)`
- **رُوَیْدَ** — PDF sirf "امر حاضر ke معنی" — actual gloss "zara rukoo, slow down" meri taraf se. `(meri taraf se gloss — "zara rukoo": book sirf "امر حاضر ka معنی" kehta)`
- **مَنْ** = "kaun" (relative/interrogative) — common pronoun, gloss derivable from Asma-e-Mausoolah context.
- **ذَا** = "yeh" (demonstrative) — common, gloss derivable from Ism Ishara context.
- **اَحَدَ عَشَرَ** — already taught p-11 (Bina'i Murakkab).

**Asma-e-Mausoolah aur Asma-e-Ishara**: yeh do **major categories** hain Ism Ghair Mutamakkin ki — magar book ne yahan **specific Asma list nahi di** (e.g., الَّذِيْ، الَّتِيْ for Mausoolah, ya هٰذَا، ذٰلِكَ for Ishara). Sirf concept introduce kiya. **Forward-ref Bab/Sarf**.

### Yaad rakho (page ke 5 mukhya cheezein)

1. **Mu'arrab kaun? Sirf 2 categories**: (a) **Ism Mutamakkin** (with tarkeeb condition) + (b) **Fi'l Muzaari** (with noon jam' mu'annath/ta'keed se khali condition). Baqi sab Mabni.
2. **Mutamakkin ka maa'na = "jagah dena"** (Block 3): yeh Ism اعراب ko jagah deta hai, isi liye yeh "place-giver" Mu'arrab hai.
3. **Mabni-al-Asl ki 3 qismein**: Fi'l Maazi + Amr Haazir Ma'roof + tamam Huroof. Yeh **"original Mabni"** hain.
4. **Ism Ghair Mutamakkin = Mabni-al-Asl ka مشابہ**. Resemblance 4 tareeqon se: (a) **meaning** se (Ayna, Hayhaata, Ruwayda), (b) **need-dependency** se (Mausoolah, Ishara), (c) **3 letter se kam** brevity se (Man, Dha), (d) Original mein **Harf shamil** thi (Ahada Ashara).
5. **Asma-e-Mausoolah aur Asma-e-Ishara** ki yahan **specific list nahi** — sirf categories introduce hue. Tafseel aage Bab mein.
