#### Hissa D — 14 Zameer Marfu' Muttasil (Block 3 + Table 1)

Tareef: "Faa'il ki wo ضمیر jo Fi'l se mili ho (attached)."

Misaal: ضَرَبْتُ = "Main ne maara" — yahaan تُ zameer hai jo Fi'l (ضَرَبْ) ke saath chipki hui hai. تُ separate likha nahi gaya — Fi'l ke part ban gaya.

Book ne 14 forms ka complete paradigm (gardaan) PDF par dikhaya. Table reading order right-to-left (Urdu/Arabic convention), aur internal order: Mutakallim → Mukhatab → Ghaayb (Block 6 footnote ke 3 persons ki tarteeb).

Table 1 ka structural breakdown:

| Cell # | Form | Person + Number + Gender | Tarjuma (mera gloss — book mein nahi) |
|--------|------|---------------------------|-----------------------------------------|
| 1 | ضَرَبْتُ | Mutakallim — singular | "Main ne maara" |
