#### Hissa C — Block 3: حَیْثُ tafseel

Tareef (Block 3 PDF): *"اَکْثَر جُمْلَہ کی طرف مضاف ہوتا ہے"*

Yaani حَیْثُ ki khaasiyat — akser (mostly) ek complete jumla ki taraf Mudaf hota hai (single word ki taraf NAHI, jaisa عِنْدَ + اَلْمَالُ ki tarah).

Misaal (PDF Block 3):
- اِجْلِسْ حَیْثُ زَیْدٌ جَالِسٌ — "Baith jahaan Zayd baitha hua hai"
- Tafseer (book ka apna): یَعْنِی اِجْلِسْ مَکَانَ جُلُوْسِ زَیْدٍ — "yaani: Zayd ke baithne ki jagah par baith"

Word breakdown *(meri taraf se grammar gloss)*:
- اِجْلِسْ — Fi'l Amr "baith"
- حَیْثُ — Zarf-e-Makaan Mabni damma, complete jumla "زَیْدٌ جَالِسٌ" ki taraf Mudaf
- زَیْدٌ جَالِسٌ — Jumla Ismiya (Mubtada Zayd + Khabar Jaalisun — "Zayd baitha hua") = Mudaf Ilayh of حَیْثُ
- Tafseer: مَکَانَ جُلُوْسِ زَیْدٍ — "Zayd ke baithne ki jagah" (Mudaf-Mudaf-Ilayh equivalent)

Pedagogical note *(meri taraf se observation; book ne explicit nahi kahi)*: حَیْثُ ki yeh khaasiyat unique hai is qism mein — baqi Zarf-e-Makaan items single Asma ki taraf Mudaf hote (jaisa عِنْدَ زَیْدٍ), magar حَیْثُ pura jumla apna Mudaf Ilayh banata.

#### Hissa D — Block 4-5: قُدَّامْ/خَلْفْ + تَحْتُ/فَوْقُ — Mudaf Ilayh poshida pattern

قُدَّامْ و خَلْفْ (Block 4 PDF):
- Misaal: قَامَ النَّاسُ قُدَّامَ وَخَلْفَ — "Log aage aur peeche khade hue"
- Tafseer (book ka): یَعْنِی قُدَّامَہٗ وَخَلْفَہٗ — "yaani: us ke aage aur us ke peeche" (Mudaf Ilayh "هٗ" — third-person — poshida tha)

Word breakdown *(meri taraf se grammar gloss)*:
- قَامَ — Fi'l Maazi "khade hue"
- النَّاسُ — Fa'il "log"
- قُدَّامَ وَخَلْفَ — Zarf Mabni alal-fath, Mudaf Ilayh "هٗ" poshida thi
- Tafseer mein هٗ explicit dikhta hai (قُدَّامَہٗ + خَلْفَہٗ)

تَحْتُ و فَوْقُ (Block 5 PDF):
- Misaal: جَلَسَ زَیْدٌ تَحْتُ وَصَعِدَ عَمْرٌو فَوْقُ — "Zayd niche baitha aur 'Amr upar chada"
- Tafseer (book ka): اَيْ تَحْتَ الشَّجَرَۃِ وَفَوْقَ الشَّجَرَۃِ مَثَلًا — "i.e., darakhat ke niche aur darakhat ke upar — for example"

Word breakdown *(meri taraf se grammar gloss)*:
- جَلَسَ زَیْدٌ تَحْتُ — "Zayd niche baitha" (تَحْتُ Mabni damma, Mudaf Ilayh poshida)
- صَعِدَ عَمْرٌو فَوْقُ — "'Amr upar chada" (فَوْقُ Mabni damma, Mudaf Ilayh poshida)
- Tafseer mein darakhat (الشَّجَرَۃِ) explicit dikhta — Mudaf Ilayh sample
- مَثَلًا ("for example") — book bata raha ke darakhat sirf ek possibility hai, asli Mudaf Ilayh context-specific hota

Cross-pattern observation *(meri taraf se; book ne explicit yeh comparison nahi kiya)*: Blocks 1, 4, 5 sab same pattern dikhate hain — Zarf akhir mein damma/fath (Mabni form), Mudaf Ilayh metakallim ki niyat mein (poshida), aur book apne tafseer mein implicit Mudaf Ilayh dikhati. #### Hissa F — Block 7: أَیْنَ و اَنّٰی (3 uses) + 3rd Quran ayah

أَیْنَ + اَنّٰی — book unhe pair karta. 3 distinct usesUse 1 — استفہام (where?):

Use 3 — اَنّٰی کَیْفَ ke ma'na mein (when after fi'l):
- Condition: *"جب کہ فعل کے بعد آئے"* — Fi'l ke baad aane par اَنّٰی کَیْفَ-equivalent ban jaata.

Cross-context — جزم concept *(meri taraf se Sarf observation; book ne yahaan explicit nahi di)*: Use 2 (shart) ki misaalein تَجْلِسْ أَجْلِسْ + تَذْهَبْ اَذْهَبْ — dono jumlay mein dono Fi'l Muzaari sukoon par hain. ### Yaad rakho (key points)

1. قَبْلُ/بَعْدُ tafseer ka khaatma (Section 18 + 19): Quran ayah tafseer اَيْ مِنْ قَبْلِ کُلِّ شَيْءٍ وَمِنْ بَعْدِ کُلِّ شَيْءٍ (har cheez se pehle aur har cheez ke baad).
2. 2 nayi قَبْلُ/بَعْدُ misaalein (Block 1): اَنَا حَاضِرٌ مِنْ قَبْلُ (Mudaf Ilayh "كَ" poshida) + مَتٰی تَجِیْئُنَا بَعْدُ (Mudaf Ilayh "هٰذَا" poshida).
3. Zarf-e-Makaan sub-qism (b) ka shuru — 9-item list (Block 2): حَیْثُ، قُدَّامْ، خَلْفُ، تَحْتُ، فَوْقُ، عِنْدَ، أَیْنَ، لَدٰی، لَدُنْ.
4. حَیْثُ (Block 3) — akser jumla ki taraf Mudaf (unique — baqi single ism ki taraf). Misaal اِجْلِسْ حَیْثُ زَیْدٌ جَالِسٌ.
5. قُدَّامْ/خَلْفْ + تَحْتُ/فَوْقُ Mudaf Ilayh poshida pattern (Blocks 4+5): Zarf akhir mein damma/fath jab Mudaf Ilayh metakallim ki niyat mein. Misaalein: قَامَ النَّاسُ قُدَّامَ وَخَلْفَ + جَلَسَ زَیْدٌ تَحْتُ وَصَعِدَ عَمْرٌو فَوْقُ.
6. عِنْدَ (Block 6): simple misaal اَلْمَالُ عِنْدَ زَیْدٍ — yahaan Mudaf Ilayh explicit (زَیْدٍ kasra), Mabni alal-fath rahti.
7. أَیْنَ + اَنّٰی ki 3 uses (Block 7): (i) Istefham (kahaan?) (ii) Shart (jahaan) (iii) اَنّٰی = کَیْفَ-equivalent jab Fi'l ke baad aaye. Section 18 ki کَیْفَ (حال) ka leverage.
8. 3rd Quran ayah (Block 7): ﴿فَأْتُوْا حَرْثَکُمْ أَنّٰی شِئْتُمْ﴾ — book ne "کَقَوْلِہٖ تَعَالٰی" attribute kiya (Allah ka qol).: preserve book attribution, Surah/ayat number NAHI add karte memory se.
9. لَدٰی/لَدُنْ tareef start (Block 8): عِنْدَ ke ma'na mein, magar farq yeh ke عِنْدَ mein qabza + milk kaafi (physical presence zaroori nahi). Tareef truncated — p-25 par لَدٰی ki specific farq + misaal khaatma.
10. Cross-context — earlier-defined terms ka natural use: Block 1 mein مَتٰی (Section 18) + Block 7 mein کَیْفَ (Section 18) re-use ho rahe bina re-definition ke. Yeh book ka good pedagogical pattern.
11. Cross-context — jussive (Majzoom) pattern: Block 7 ki shart misaalein تَجْلِسْ، أَجْلِسْ، تَذْهَبْ، اَذْهَبْ sab Fi'l Muzaari sukoon par (jussive form, Sarf concept). Section 18 ki مَتٰی تَصُمْ اَصُمْ jaisa pattern. Book ne abhi jussive ki terminology nahi di — sirf misaalein.
12. 3 consecutive pages mein Quran ayahs (p-22, p-23, p-24) — sab cases mein book ka attribution level exactly preserve kiya, memory se Surah/ayat plant NAHI ki.) ka 3rd successful application.
