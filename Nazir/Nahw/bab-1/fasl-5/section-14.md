### Aaj ki kahani (page ka khulasa — pedagogical narrative)

#### Hissa B — Zameer Mansoob Muttasil (Table 3) + Tarkeeb critical insight

Tareef: Maf'ool ki wo zameer jo Fi'l se mili ho (attached).

Key insight from Table 3: Sab forms ضَرَبَ- se shuru hote — yaani Fa'il (هُوَ implicit) constant. Sirf Maf'ool varies (نِيْ، كَ، كُمَا... هُنَّ). Yeh Table 1 (Marfu' Muttasil) ka mirror — wahaan Fa'il vary kar raha tha, yahaan Fa'il constant + Maf'ool vary.

Tarkeeb walkthrough (Block 3) ka asal kaam — book ne ضَرَبَنِيْ ko ungla pakad kar parse karke dikhaaya:

| Element | Role | PDF wording |
|---------|------|-------------|
| ضَرَبَ | Fi'l Maazi | "ضَرَبَ فعل" |
| (hidden) هُوَ | Fa'il | "اس میں ضمیر هُوَ کا فاعل" |
| نون وقایہ = نِ | "Protective noon" between Fi'l + Mutakallim ya | "'نون وقایہ کا'" |
| یْ | Mutakallim zameer | "'یا' ضمیر متکلم مفعول" |
| Total | Jumla Fi'liya Khabariya | "جملہ فعلیہ خبریہ ہوا" |

Naya buniyadi termنون وقایہ (Noon Wiqayah) — Fi'l ke aakhir aur Mutakallim ya ke darmiyaan aane wala "protective" noon. Forward-ref Sarf — book ne yahan sirf naam diya, tafseel nahi. Maine bhi sirf naam preserve kiya — no definition fabrication.

Maf'ool bih — Tarkeeb mein use kiya magar book ne pehle formally define nahi kiya. Forward-ref — possibly p-08 ke "Maf'ool" / Fasl 1 ka mention sufficient.

#### Hissa C — Zameer Mansoob Munfasil (Table 4) — اِیَّا series

Tareef: Maf'ool ki wo zameer jo Fi'l se جدا (separated/independent) hoti.

Key insight from Table 4: Sab forms اِیَّا- prefix se shuru hote (similar to how Marfu' Munfasil had no common prefix — Mutakallim used اَنَا, Ghaayb used هُوَ etc.). Yahaan independence ka tareeqa اِیَّا + person-suffix hai.

اِیَّاكَ = "sirf Aap ki / sirf tujh ko" — Maf'ool Mansoob Munfasil ki classic shakal. Surah Fatiha mein اِیَّاكَ form widely-recited hai (verbatim Quran quote NAHI di — Islamic content rule: Quran sirf book ke PDF se quote karna, memory se nahi). *(yeh form-recognition meri taraf se — book ne yahan Surah Fatiha ka reference nahi diya, sirf paradigm form di)*Note: Forms #4 + #7 (اِیَّاكُمَا) dual symmetry — same as p-18 tables.

#### Hissa D — Zameer Majroor Muttasil ka split: 2 sub-types

Block 6 ka critical structural point: Zameer Majroor Muttasil bhi 14 hain, magar yeh 2 alag tareeqon se Majroor hoti:

| Type | Trigger (Amil) | Misaal series |
|------|----------------|----------------|
| A: حرف جر داخل ہو | Harf-e-Jarr (e.g., لِ، بِ، فِيْ) Ism par lage | لِيْ، لَكَ، لَهُ... (Table 5) |
| B: مضاف داخل ہو | Ism Mudaf ke baad as Mudaf Ilayh | دَارِيْ، دَارُكَ، دَارُهُ... (Table 6) |

Both types ke 14 forms — total 28 forms.

Why 2 sub-types? Kyunke Majroor state ek noun ko 2 alag situations mein lag sakti:
1. Harf-e-Jarr ke baad (e.g., "fee bayti" — "in my house" — bayti = Majroor by فِيْ)
2. Mudaf Ilayh ke role mein (e.g., "ghulamuhu" — "his slave" — hu = Majroor by Mudaf ghulam)

*(yeh "2 alag situations" framing meri taraf se — book ne explicit yeh contrast nahi diya, sirf 2 tables ke saath naam mention kiye)*
